My Style:

What I value:

	and I value their personality as well. I am not a perfect person, but I strive to be a hard-working person however,

	Even though we don’t have the patience for it at the moment or truly care for it we should always try and give our 100% when we are working on 
	the things we are working on.
How to best communicate with me:

	This way, you will undoubtedly be able to get my full attention

What people misunderstand about me:
